What We Do

At Retro Electro, we combine modern electrical solutions with old-school values. Pricing

First-Hour Minimum Charge: $130 (includes labour and travel)
Additional Hours: $90 per hour

Free Quotes & estimates without a site visit done for most jobs.

Travel Charges
If you're located in Hastings or Napier, travel fees are already included in the First-Hour Minimum Charge.
For jobs 10km or further from Hastings or Napier town centres, a travel fee of $1.50 per km applies in addition to the standard hourly rate.

After-Hours & Emergency Rates
Our standard working hours are Monday to Friday, 8 AM - 5 PM.
For after-hours, weekends, and public holidays:
First Hour: $270
Additional Hours: $130 per hour
